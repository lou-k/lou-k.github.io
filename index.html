Lou Kratz

is a huge nerd.

Philadelphia, PA

I’m a research engineer specializing in computer vision and machine learning. I spent most of my time on tech-transfer: figuring out how to build cutting edge research from the lab into real-world applications.

Currently, I work Curalate where they let me build cool apps. Working at a startup lets me rapidly switch between various roles including researcher, software engineer, and data scientist.

Prior to Curalate, I received my PhD from Drexel University under the mentorship of Ko Nishino. Periodically I teach Computer Vision and Computational Photography at Drexel.

When I’m not working, I enjoy photography, cooking, homebrewing, and fatherhood.
